Western Region Grammateus Candidates

Western Region Grammateus Daun Fizer

I am a Sr. Tax analyst for a major financial company and have been in the financial industry for over 35 years. I have a Bachelor’s degree in Behavioral Science as well as an MBA. Since becoming one of the pRHOud ladies of Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority Inc. , I have held several leadership positions on both the local and regional level. I currently serve in the role of Regional Grammateus as well as the chapter Tamiochus. I have always had a passion for numbers and no matter what organization I am a part of, I eventually find myself serving in a financial capacity. My goal for the next term, if elected, is to continue to be a resource to the local Grammati as well as the affiliates. As we are hosting elections in every facet of the organization, there will be new Grammati as well as new financial secretaries in the affiliate orgs. I intend to be available to assist them as they transition into their new roles and to be a continued resource for those that are returning.
