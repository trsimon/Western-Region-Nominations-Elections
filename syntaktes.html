Candidates for Western Region Syntaktes

Western Region Syntaktes Michelle Aldridge

Syntaktes Aldridge was raised in Milpitas, CA. She earned a Bachelor’s in Business Management and Master’s in Business Administration at San Jose State University where she joined Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc. Soror Aldridge is married to her husband Cristopher and they have three sons-Jason, Ty, and Shane. Soror Aldridge has a long-standing career as a Human Resources professional and is a Senior HR Business Partner for Juniper Networks. Soror Aldridge currently serves as Regional Syntaktes for the Western Region of Sigma Gamma Rho. She is a Ruby/Life member and has held multiple leadership positions at the local, regional and national level over her 30-year tenure. She is a charter member of the Silicon Valley alumnae chapter - Iota Beta Sigma, of which she continues to be an active member.
